\makerubrichead{Research Interests}
%
My research lies at the intersection of \textbf{robotics}, \textbf{machine learning}, and \textbf{control} engineering. I develop algorithms for real-time trajectory planning, control, and estimation of autonomous robots, with a focus on \textbf{mobile robots} and ground vehicles in uncertain, dynamic environments. I like to integrate prior knowledge of the robot dynamics into learning-based methods, such as neural networks, to enhance their \textbf{generalization to unseen scenarios} with minimal training data.
